# SEO Audit

Use the `seo-specialist` agent to perform a comprehensive SEO audit and optimization analysis for:

$ARGUMENTS

## Quick Examples

- `/seo-audit https://example.com` - Full technical SEO audit of a website
- `/seo-audit src/pages` - Audit page structure for SEO best practices
- `/seo-audit --keywords "react tutorial"` - Keyword research and competition analysis
- `/seo-audit --schema` - Review and recommend structured data implementation
