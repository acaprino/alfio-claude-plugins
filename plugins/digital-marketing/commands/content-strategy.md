# Content Strategy

Use the `content-marketer` agent to develop a content strategy and marketing plan for:

$ARGUMENTS

## Quick Examples

- `/content-strategy "B2B SaaS product launch"` - Full content strategy for a product launch
- `/content-strategy --audit src/blog` - Audit existing content and identify gaps
- `/content-strategy --calendar Q1 2026` - Generate editorial calendar for a quarter
- `/content-strategy --competitor "competitor.com"` - Competitive content analysis
